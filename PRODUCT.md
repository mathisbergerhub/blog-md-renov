# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Homeowners and property owners in Haute-Savoie, Savoie, and Pays de Gex planning renovation work for windows, shutters, pergolas, doors, gates, insulation, and local grant applications.

## Product Purpose

The MD Renov blog helps visitors understand options, budgets, grants, and local constraints before asking for a quote. Success means a clear, trustworthy path from reading an article or category page to requesting a quote.

## Brand Personality

Local expertise, practical confidence, careful craft. The tone is clear, serious, warm, and direct.

## Anti-references

Generic AI SaaS layouts, decorative glassmorphism, oversized rounded cards, vague marketing copy, flashy animations that slow repeated navigation, and inaccessible mobile menus.

## Design Principles

- Mobile navigation must feel calm, immediate, and trustworthy.
- Motion should clarify state changes, not decorate.
- Preserve MD Renov red and the existing editorial typography.
- Keep content readable and action paths obvious.
- Prefer semantic HTML and accessible controls.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Aim for WCAG AA basics: keyboard-operable navigation, visible focus, reduced-motion support, meaningful link labels, contrast-safe text, and no scroll traps.
